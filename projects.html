W.I.P. Geometrical Modelisation Quadtree - Unity

I developed a recursive algorithm to create a 3D volumetric representation of spheres using spatial enumeration. The approach involves subdividing a bounding box into smaller cubes, allowing for efficient representation and manipulation of 3D objects. The algorithm supports the addition of multiple spheres, dynamically adjusting the bounding box to encompass all objects.

AI pathfinding A* & Dijkstra - Unity

The goal of this game is to capture as many tiles on the grid as possible. Two competing algorithms, A* and Dijkstra, alternate turns, each attempting to claim tiles by expanding their paths. A tile can be recaptured by the opponent if it's not currently owned. The challenge lies in strategically navigating the grid to maximize your captured tiles while potentially reclaiming tiles from your competitor. The game tests the efficiency and adaptability of both algorithms in real-time.

LightUp - Custom Engine

Light Up is a single-player puzzle game in the Simon Tatham's Puzzle Collection. The goal is to illuminate all blank squares on a grid by placing light bulbs on some of them. Each light bulb lights up its square and those in the same row or column unless blocked by black squares. The black squares may be numbered, indicating how many light bulbs must be adjacent to them. The game has four main types of squares: blank, light bulbs, black walls (numbered or not), and marks. The objective is to light all non-black squares, avoid lighting one light by another, and satisfy the number of lights around numbered black squares.
